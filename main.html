    IMBU students partner with BrandSphere Asia, a regional branding consultancy, to design integrated marketing campaigns for lifestyle brands expanding into ASEAN. The program includes client briefings, market research, and campaign pitching

    In a joint initiative with Nuvanta Retail Group, students join a simulated business challenge involving new product development, pricing strategy, and cross-border logistics. Finalists present their strategies to the company’s leadership panel
